    <h1 class="jade-section-title">AI systems for climate governance and environmental intelligence.</h1>
    <p class="jade-section-lead">My work develops artificial intelligence systems that transform satellite observations, climate pathways and sustainability data into actionable intelligence for public policy, emissions mitigation and environmental governance.</p>
    <div class="jade-grid">
      <div class="jade-card">

        <ul><li>Climate scenario vetting</li><li>Policy intelligence</li><li>Review-load indicators</li><li>Transition-burden analysis</li></ul>
      </div>
      <div class="jade-card">
        <h3>Earth Observation & Remote Sensing</h3>
        <p>Computer vision and geospatial AI for satellite imagery, industrial activity monitoring, methane emissions and infrastructure systems.</p>
        <ul><li>Satellite infrastructure mapping</li><li>Methane intelligence</li><li>Object detection</li><li>Open-vocabulary Earth observation</li></ul>
      </div>
      <div class="jade-card">
        <h3>Sustainability Intelligence Systems</h3>
        <p>Decision-support systems connecting machine learning, mathematical modeling and policy indicators for sustainable development.</p>
        <ul><li>SDG monitoring</li><li>Environmental indicators</li><li>Automated evidence synthesis</li><li>Operational policy analytics</li></ul>
